ABOUT US


TECHNE, an inter-collegiate technical festival organized by the department of Computer Science and ASSET, is a one-day festival featuring various events and talks by esteemed personals. It is an embodiment of entertainment and technology which aspires to promote the diversification of information technology to the world. The program will comprise of events that are in alliance with the technical students and the information society. The event will encourage a wide array of competitions and tournaments.

This full-day program will flaunt an abundance of events and exhibitions including Jail Break, Spider Sense, VR Station, Electro Arcade and many more. TECHNE strives to congregate students from distinct backgrounds to celebrate the diversity of technology and create an ethereal experience. Join us on our unprecedented adventure for an awe-inspiring experience.
